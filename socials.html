Club Traditions

Annual Awards Night

Our prestigious awards night celebrates the season's achievements with dinner, presentations, and recognition of players, volunteers, and supporters.

A beloved tradition bringing together families for games, food, fun, and exhibition matches showcasing all age groups.

Wrapping up each season with a relaxed barbecue where players, families, and supporters share stories and look forward to next season.
