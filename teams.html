Junior Development Program

Ages 5-16 | Skills development & competitive pathways

Our Junior Development Program introduces children to hockey in a fun, supportive environment. Our qualified coaches focus on fundamental skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Our program follows Hockey Australia's Hookin2Hockey framework and progresses to competitive leagues for older juniors.

  • Two age divisions: Under 12 and Under 16
  • Wednesday skills sessions + Saturday competition
  • All equipment provided for beginners
  • Pathways to state development programs

Masters Hockey

Social and competitive hockey for players 35+

Our Masters program welcomes players aged 35+ who want to enjoy hockey in a social yet competitive environment. The focus is on fitness, friendship, and the love of the game, with flexibility for those balancing sport with work and family commitments. Both experienced players and those returning to hockey are welcome.

  • Training focused on fitness and skills maintenance
  • Flexible attendance - play as your schedule permits
  • Opportunities to compete in regional masters tournaments
