Play for Grampians Hockey

Start your hockey journey with us today

Whether you're new to hockey or an experienced player, we have a team for you. Our inclusive environment welcomes players of all ages and skill levels. From junior development programmes to competitive senior teams, you'll find your place at Grampians Hockey Club.

  • Loan equipment provided for beginners
  • Professional coaching for all levels
  • Flexible training schedules
  • Pathways to state and national levels

Volunteer With Us

Help our club thrive through volunteering

Volunteers are the backbone of our club. From coaching and umpiring to event organisation and committee roles, there are many ways to contribute. Share your skills, meet new people, and make a positive impact on our hockey community.

  • Flexible time commitments to suit your schedule
  • Training and support provided for all roles
  • Great for building skills and experience
  • Social volunteer appreciation events
See Volunteer Roles

Coaching

Lead training sessions and develop players' skills. Coaching accreditation provided.

Time: 2-4 hours/week

Umpiring

Officiate games and ensure fair play. Umpiring courses available.

Time: 3-6 hours/week

Event Organisation

Help plan and run club events, fundraisers, and social activities.

Time: Flexible

Committee Roles

Join our committee to help guide the club's direction and operations.

Time: 2-4 hours/month

Support Our Club

Help us grow through sponsorship and support

Our sponsors and supporters play a crucial role in helping us provide quality facilities, equipment, and programmes for our members. Whether you're a local business looking for sponsorship opportunities or an individual wanting to support our club, there are many ways to get involved.

  • Multiple sponsorship packages available
  • Marketing benefits and community recognition
  • Tax-deductible donations welcome
  • Social membership for supporters
