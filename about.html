Our Club Story

Celebrating decades of hockey excellence and community spirit in the Grampians region

Welcome to Grampians Hockey

Since our founding in the 1970s, Grampians Hockey Club has been the heart of hockey in our region. What started as a small group of hockey enthusiasts has grown into a vibrant community club with teams across junior, senior, and masters competitions.

Our club prides itself on creating a welcoming environment for players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're looking to compete at the highest level, learn the game, or stay active in a friendly atmosphere, you'll find a place at Grampians Hockey Club.

Our Mission

To develop and promote hockey in the Grampians region, providing quality playing, training, and social experiences for all members while building community connections through sport.

Club History Timeline

1976 - Founding Year

A group of local enthusiasts establishes Grampians Hockey Club with one men's team competing in the regional competition.

1983 - Women's Programme

Formation of our first women's team, marking the beginning of gender equality at the club.

1992 - Junior Development

Launch of our junior development programme, introducing hockey to schools across the region.

2005 - Home Ground

Establishment of our dedicated home ground with modern hockey facilities.

2015 - Expansion

Club expansion adds teams to regional and metropolitan competitions.

Today

A thriving community hub supporting players, families, and supporters.
